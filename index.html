About the Mod

GTA San Andreas Stories, also known as GTA San Andreas Stories: The Return of Ryder or GTA San Andreas Stories: 1987, is a fan-made mod by babamohammed2022. It’s still being developed and tries to bring back the feel of the GTA Stories games.

This mod uses DYOM 8.1 and follows Ryder’s return to Los Santos in 1987, where he starts new adventures and meets old friends again.

For more details, check out our Discord server: Discord Server

Note: If you use the Essential Modpack, remove the Widescreen mod from Modloader to avoid crashes. Moreover, SAMP is not compatible with the modpack.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is the setting of the mod?

The mod is set in 1987 in the fictional city of Los Santos, a sprawling metropolis on the brink of chaos. Gangs, including the iconic Grove Street Families, battle for control and survival amidst rising threats.

Q2: Who is the main protagonist in the storyline?

The main protagonist is Lance "Ryder" Wilson, a loyal member of the Grove Street Families. After a period in Vice City, Ryder returns to Los Santos to strengthen the gang in the absence of his childhood friend CJ.

Q3: Why did CJ leave Los Santos?

CJ, also known as Carl Johnson, left Los Santos due to the death of his brother, Brian Johnson. The loss of his brother led CJ to depart for Liberty City, leaving the responsibility of leading the Grove Street Families to his brother Sweet and others.

Q4: Are there multiple chapters in the storyline?

Yes, the storyline is divided into 3 chapters, filled with dynamic gang encounters across Los Santos.

Q5: Are there any side missions?

Yes, three side missions have been added so far:

  • Pizza Delivery (similar to GTA VC/GTA LCS)
  • Trash Dash (similar to GTA LCS)
  • Drug Bust Operation (WIP)

Q6: Can you change outfits?

Yes, there are 11 outfit options (+ a bonus outfit), and you can find clothing pickups around the map, similar to GTA Vice City.

Q7: What kind of weapons and vehicles are available?

The mod features weapons from multiple Rockstar Games titles, such as Manhunt, GTA Vice City Stories, and GTA Liberty City Stories, along with new weapon pickups scattered across the map. You’ll also see vehicles from Vice City and Vice City Stories along with a few new ones.

Q8: Does the game have new characters?

Yes, iconic characters like Tommy Vercetti, Ken Rosenberg, and 8-Ball make appearances in the mod to make it more interesting.

Q9: Are there any new features or mechanics introduced in the mod?

The mod introduces new cheat codes, along with the original GTA SA cheats. There are also '80s atmosphere updates with billboards, timecyc, and more, plus a Stories-style HUD and animations for a PSP-styled gameplay experience.

Q10: Are there any map changes?

Yes, you’ll notice various map updates, including new interiors for Ryder’s house, the FIDL store, and other parts of Los Santos.

Q11: How can I become a tester?

Just ask the owner. You can become a tester by expressing your interest and providing regular feedback. We are always looking for dedicated testers to improve the mod.

Q12: Will the mod be compatible with other GTA San Andreas mods?

We try to ensure compatibility, but the mod is not compatible with SA-MP and Essentials Pack, especially the sidescreen mod. It is discouraged to download mods outside the modpack unless the owner specifies otherwise.

Q13: How can I report bugs or issues with the mod?

If you encounter any bugs or issues, please report them through our official channels. You can use our Discord server or message the owner directly. Providing detailed information will help us resolve issues faster.

Requirements

Ready to jump into Los Santos? Here’s what your PC needs:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Dual-Core or similar
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics
  • Storage: 3 GB Free Space
  • Additional Requirements: Clean copy of GTA San Andreas, GTA SA EXE 1.0 US version, CLEO 4 and DYOM 8.1

Ensure your system meets these requirements to enjoy a smooth gaming experience in San Andreas!
