About the Mod

GTA San Andreas Stories, also known as GTA San Andreas Stories: The Return of Ryder or GTA San Andreas Stories: 1987, is a fan-made mod by babamohammed2022. It’s still being developed and tries to bring back the feel of the GTA Stories games.

This mod uses DYOM 8.1 and follows Ryder’s return to Los Santos in 1987, where he starts new adventures and meets old friends again.

For more details, check out our Discord server: Discord Server

Note: If you use the Essential Modpack, remove the Widescreen mod from Modloader to avoid crashes. Moreover, SAMP is not compatible with the modpack.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is the setting of the mod?

It is set in 1987 in the fictional city of Los Santos, a sprawling metropolis on the brink of chaos. Gangs, including the iconic Grove Street Families, battle for control and survival amidst rising threats.

Q2: Who is the main protagonist in the storyline?

The main protagonist is Lance "Ryder" Wilson, a loyal member of the Grove Street Families. After a period in Vice City, Ryder returns to Los Santos to strengthen the gang in the absence of his childhood friend CJ.

Q3: Why did CJ leave Los Santos?

CJ, also known as Carl Johnson, left Los Santos because of the death of his brother, Brian Johnson. The loss of his brother led CJ to depart for Liberty City, leaving the responsibility of leading the Grove Street Families to his brother Sweet and others.

Q4: Are there multiple chapters in the storyline?

Yes, the storyline is divided into 3 chapters.

Q5: Are there any side missions?

Yes, three side missions have been added so far:

  • Pizza Delivery (similar to GTA VC/GTA LCS)

  • Trash Dash (similar to GTA LCS)

  • Drug Bust Operation (WIP)

Q6: Who are the main antagonists in the game?

The primary antagonist is Victor "Viper" Mendez, a cunning and ruthless drug lord with roots in a South American cartel. Viper seeks to expand his drug empire in Los Santos and views Ryder as a significant obstacle.

Q7: Are there any new features or mechanics introduced in the mod?

Yes, the mod introduces several new features including unique missions, additional gang dynamics, and enhanced gameplay mechanics that build on the original GTA San Andreas experience.

Q8: How can I become a tester?

Just ask the owner. Most of the time, you can become one by simply expressing your interest and willingness to help. We are always looking for dedicated testers to improve the mod. Please note that you need to be active and provide regular feedback.

Q9: Will the mod be compatible with other GTA San Andreas mods?

We try to make the mod as compatible as possible. However, it is specifically not compatible with SA-MP and the Essentials Pack, especially the sidescreen mod. We strongly discourage downloading other mods that are not part of the modpack unless explicitly permitted by the mod owner.

Q10: How can I report bugs or issues with the mod?

If you encounter any bugs or issues while using the mod, please report them through our official channels. You can use our Discord server, or message the owner directly. Providing detailed information about the issue will help us resolve it more efficiently.

Requirements

Ready to jump into Los Santos? Here’s what your PC needs:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Dual-Core or similar
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics
  • Storage: 3 GB Free Space
  • Additional Requirements: Clean copy of GTA San Andreas, GTA SA EXE 1.0 US version, CLEO and DYOM 8.1

Ensure your system meets these requirements to enjoy a smooth gaming experience in San Andreas!
